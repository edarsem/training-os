Training load vocabulary:
- Load = TL (training load). 150 TL is a salient session. 300 TL is a major session.
- Shape = CTL (long-term fitness trend). Under 60 is low (almost no training). 60-100 is a good base. 100-150 is a strong shape.
- Stress = ATL (acute load, short-term).
- Stress ratio = ACWR (acute/chronic load ratio). Over 100% means increasing load. Over 150% can be risky if sustained. Race freshness: ~70%.
